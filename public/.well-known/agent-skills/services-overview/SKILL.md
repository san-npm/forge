# Openletz services overview

Openletz is a Luxembourg AI agency operated by Commit Media S.à r.l. (RCS
B276192). The tagline is "Websites that think, move & transact." AI agents and
automation are the front door; websites and digital growth are the body; Web3 is
secondary depth used only when it genuinely helps.

## What Openletz builds

- **AI agents & automation** (the front door): conversational AI agents,
  chatbots, and workflow automation that handle support, lead capture, and
  internal operations. Hosted in Europe, built with GDPR and the EU AI Act in
  mind.
- **Websites & shops** (the body): fast, multilingual Next.js websites and
  e-commerce, designed to convert and to be readable by both people and AI
  crawlers.
- **Digital growth**: SEO, AEO (answer-engine optimisation), analytics, and
  conversion work around the sites and agents above.
- **Web3 / on-chain** (secondary depth): added only when an on-chain component
  is the right tool for the job, not by default.

## SME Package co-funding

For eligible Luxembourg SMEs, Openletz helps claim the **SME Package**: 70% state
co-funding on eligible project costs (EUR 3,000-25,000, reimbursed after
delivery, subject to eligibility and Ministry of the Economy approval). See the
`sme-funding-estimate` skill for a quick estimate, or
https://openletz.ai/sme-package.

## Matching a need to a service

- "I want to automate support / capture leads / answer FAQs" -> AI agents &
  automation.
- "I need a new website or online shop" -> websites & shops.
- "I have a site but no traffic or conversions" -> digital growth.
- "I want tokens / on-chain payments / provenance" -> Web3, if it truly fits.
- "I'm a Luxembourg SME and want to reduce the cost" -> SME Package co-funding.

## Honest scope

Openletz is a small, founder-operated studio. It does not claim to do everything;
the goal of any conversation is to scope one well-defined project. To go further,
use the `start-project` skill or contact https://openletz.ai/contact.
