# Start a project with Openletz

Openletz is a Luxembourg AI agency (Commit Media S.à r.l., RCS B276192). Its one
job is to turn a clear need into a well-scoped project. This skill explains how
to begin.

## What to prepare

Before reaching out, it helps to have a rough sense of:

1. **The problem.** What outcome do you want? (e.g. "automate customer support",
   "launch a multilingual shop", "improve search ranking".)
2. **Context.** Your company, sector, languages you operate in, and any existing
   site or tools.
3. **Budget and timing.** Even a rough range helps scope the work realistically.
   If you are a Luxembourg SME, ask about the SME Package (70% co-funding).
4. **Constraints.** Hosting/region requirements, GDPR and EU AI Act
   considerations, integrations.

You do not need a finished brief. A few sentences are enough to start.

## How scoping works

1. **Enquiry.** You send a short description of what you want.
2. **Scoping conversation.** Openletz clarifies the goal, suggests the right
   service (AI agents & automation, website/shop, digital growth, or Web3 if it
   fits), and flags whether SME Package co-funding could apply.
3. **Proposal.** You receive an indicative scope, timeline, and price. For
   eligible SMEs, the SME Package estimate (indicative, subject to approval) is
   included.
4. **Build & deliver.** Work proceeds in clear steps with check-ins.

Openletz is a small, founder-operated studio and takes on a limited number of
projects. The first conversation is about whether there is a good fit, with no
obligation. Funding figures are indicative and subject to eligibility and
Ministry of the Economy approval.
